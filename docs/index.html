Declan McNamara

PhD Candidate in Statistics

University of Michigan

I’m a 5th year Ph.D. candidate in statistics at the University of Michigan, advised by Dr. Jeffrey Regier. My research focuses on methods and applications for scalable approximate Bayesian inference. I’m grateful to be supported as a Graduate Research Fellow of the National Science Foundation (NSF).

Interests
